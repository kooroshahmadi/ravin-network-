# Workstations and Clients

### Workstations
**Definition:** High-performance computers designed for specialized, heavy-duty technical tasks.
*   **Key Hardware:** High RAM, Dedicated GPU, Multi-core CPU.
*   **Users:** Engineers, Editors, Scientists.
*   **vs Standard PC:** Workstations handle rendering, compiling, and scientific calculation rather than just word processing.

![[10-essential-components-of-a-productive-large-desk-setup.webp]]

### Clients
**Definition:** End-user devices that consume services.
*   **Relationship:** They initiate the connection.
*   **Examples:** Laptops, Phones, Tablets, IoT.
