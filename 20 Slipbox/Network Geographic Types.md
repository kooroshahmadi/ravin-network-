# Network Geographic Types

Networks are often classified by the physical area they cover.

### 1. Local Scopes
*   **PAN (Personal Area Network):** Range up to 10 meters (Bluetooth, USB). Personal devices.
![[99 Attachments/pan-diagram.webp]]
*   **LAN (Local Area Network):** Restricted to a single location (Home, Office floor).![[LAN Diagram.png]]
*   **WLAN (Wireless LAN):** A LAN using radio waves (Wi-Fi/2.4GHz) instead of cables.
![[WLAN Diagram.jpg]]
### 2. City/Campus Scopes
*   **CAN (Campus Area Network):** Interconnected LANs in a specific region (University, Hospital, Corp Complex).
![[CAN Diagram.png]]
*   **MAN (Metropolitan Area Network):** 1km to 50km range. Connects LANs across a city.
![[MAN Diagram.png]]
### 3. Global Scopes
*   **WAN (Wide Area Network):** Spans cities, countries, or continents.
    *   *Example:* The Internet.
    *   *Tech:* Satellites, Leased Lines, Fiber Optic.
![[WAN Diagram.png]]
### 4. Special Purpose
*   **SAN (Storage Area Network):** Dedicated high-speed network for storage devices (block-level access).
![[SAN Diagram.png]]
